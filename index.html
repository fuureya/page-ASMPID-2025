About ASMPID

Annual Scientific Meeting on Pediatric Infectious Diseases

The Annual Scientific Meeting on Pediatric Infectious Diseases (ASMPID) is a prestigious forum that brings together leading experts, clinicians, researchers, and healthcare professionals dedicated to the study and management of infectious diseases in children. This annual event serves as a platform for the exchange of the latest scientific knowledge, clinical experiences, and research innovations aimed at improving pediatric infectious disease prevention, diagnosis, and treatment.

Through keynote presentations, panel discussions, and interactive sessions, ASMPID fosters collaboration and continuous learning within the medical community. It also provides opportunities for networking and professional development, inspiring future advancements in pediatric infectious disease care and public health strategies. ASMPID is committed to enhancing child health outcomes through science, education, and collaboration.
